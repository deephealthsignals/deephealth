Directions to the Nemati Lab

The Nemati Lab is on the 5th floor (Room 579) of the Psychology and Interdisciplinary Sciences Bldg, 36 Eagle Row, 5th Floor South, Atlanta, GA 30322. Take the south elevator to the 5th floor, where the Biomedical Informatics Department is located.
